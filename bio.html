A Little Bit About Seth Kahn

I grew up in a small town in Connecticut with my father, who is a psychiatrist, my mother, who is a reading consultant, and my older sister.

I attended my local public high school, taking required courses and acquiring a particular interest in the natural sciences, especially physics. In the summers of 2012 and 2013, I developed a curiosity for computer science, and entertained that curiosity with some self study of isolated programming topics.

I now attend Tufts University, considering a degree in the realm of computation. I am considering a double degree in computer engineering and computer science, or possibly a degree just in computer science. Still, I take courses in physics to entertain my interest in the sciences.
